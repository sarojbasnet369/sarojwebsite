Online Engineering entrance preparation sites/institutes in Nepal

Published on October 16, 2024

Author: Saroj Basnet (Rank 3, IOE BE/BArch Entrance 2077)

It takes at least three months to receive the results of the grade XII annual exam. During this period, students can utilize their time for various purposes, such as engaging in creative activities, learning new skills, pursuing hobbies, or preparing for the entrance exams of various educational programs. Different colleges and universities conduct entrance exams before admitting students to their Bachelor's programs.

Pic: Tribhuvan University

Tribhuwan University, Kathmandu University, Pokhara University, and Purbanchal University are some of the renowned universities in Nepal offering various engineering courses. The Institute of Engineering (TU) has been the top choice for many students for years. There is fierce competition to get into a good engineering campus or college, in the field of one’s choice, in Nepal. Every year, thousands of students take the engineering entrance exam, but not all succeed. In 2081, more than 9,300 students participated in the IOE BE/BArch Entrance Examination, but only 6,530 passed. For many students, online engineering entrance preparation is an excellent way to improve their chances of getting into their dream college.

Benefits of Online Engineering Entrance Preparation

  • Flexibility: Access courses from anywhere, anytime, allowing you to study at your own pace and on your schedule.
  • Affordability: Online courses are far more affordable than traditional classroom (physical) courses.
  • Convenience: Study from the comfort of your home, saving time and money on transportation and accommodation.
  • Personalized Attention: Many online courses offer live chat or video conferencing with instructors and mentors for personalized help.
  • Access to Resources: Practice questions, study guides, mock tests, video tutorials, and recorded lectures are commonly available.
  • Community: Online forums and discussion boards (Facebook, Discord, Telegram groups, etc.) to connect with fellow students and share ideas.

Best Online Engineering Entrance Preparation Institutes in Nepal are listed below:

1. Clamphook Academy

Clamphook Academy is an online and physical engineering entrance preparation institute founded in 2019 by Er. Bikki Chhantyal, an alumnus of IOE Pulchowk Campus. It offers a variety of online courses for students preparing for engineering entrance exams in Nepal, including the IOE entrance exam, PSC, and CSIT entrance exams.

The courses are taught by experienced and qualified instructors who are experts in engineering entrance preparation. The academy provides a variety of resources to help students succeed, including practice questions, weekly online mock tests, study guides, and video tutorials.

Pic: Students taking weekly computer-based test.

Clamphook Academy has gained significant success in a short time, thanks to positive word-of-mouth and features like computer-based weekly mock tests and a collection of memory-based IOE past questions. Its website hosts more than 50,000 questions for practice. The teaching team consists of young, energetic, and talented past toppers, as well as teachers with decades of experience from IOE Pulchowk and other colleges.

The fee for online classes is Rs. 10,000.

If you are interested in learning more about Clamphook Academy, you can visit their website or contact them through their social media channels.

Website: www.clamphook.com

2. PEA Digitals, Engineeringdote

PEA Association Pvt. Ltd. is a leading engineering entrance preparation institute in Nepal. It was founded by Er. Laxman Pokharel, a graduate of Pulchowk Campus, in 1994. PEA Association offers a variety of online and physical courses for students preparing for engineering entrance exams in Nepal, including the IOE entrance exam, PSC, BE tuition classes, and the CSIT entrance exam. It has been helping students prepare for engineering entrance exams in Nepal for over 30 years.

Pic: PEA building

PEA Association's courses are taught by instructors with decades of experience, who are experts in engineering entrance preparation. PEA Association also offers a variety of resources to help students succeed, including practice questions, study guides, and video tutorials. PEA Engineeringdote offers more than 50,000 practice questions, and PEA Digitals provides over 350 hours of recorded lectures.

The fee is Rs. 15,000 for online classes and Rs. 7,000 for the PEA Mentors subscription.

If you are interested in learning more about PEA, you can visit their website or contact them through their social media channels.

Website: www.pea.edu.np

3. Ambition Guru

Ambition Guru is an AI-driven ed-tech platform that guides learners to be the best at anything they desire. You can prepare for the engineering entrance exam on this platform.

Apart from engineering, entrance exams for BSc. CSIT, CEE, Loksewa, SEE Bridge Course and other courses are available too.

Pic: AG building

Students can take an AI-based 'mock test' on it. Students can track their performance and see the report. The entrance exam fee on this platform is Rs 4,500.

If you are interested in learning more about Ambition Guru, you can visit their website or contact them through their social media channels. You can download its app from Playstore.

Website: Ambition Guru

4. Nepal Institute

Nepal Institute is one of the oldest BE/B. Arch (IOE) entrance preparation institutes in Nepal. They also run classes for CSIT entrance preparation. With a team of teachers having decades of experience, it provides quality education and entrance exam preparation. It is located in Maitighar, Kathmandu, Nepal.

Pic: Top ranker students with teachers

5. PI Academy

PI Academy stands as a well-known IOE Entrance Preparation Institute in Nepal. This institute's main objective is to provide a solid groundwork for students aiming to secure admission in BE, B.Arch, and B.Sc.CSIT programs across various universities. Situated in Matighar Mandala, Kathmandu, the academy was established by a team of devoted educators and former students from Pulchowk Campus. This group has garnered more than ten years of experience in the realm of entrance exam preparation. Their unwavering dedication revolves around quenching the thirst for knowledge among engineering enthusiasts, all the while making valuable contributions to nurturing skilled future engineers. This endeavor holds immense significance for the advancement and prosperity of the nation.

If you are considering online engineering entrance preparation, here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a reputable course provider: Ensure the course has a good track record.
  • Consider your budget: Choose a course that fits your financial situation.
  • Make sure the course is right for you: Some courses focus more on material teaching, while others focus on practice questions and mock exams.
  • Be prepared to put in the work: Online courses are helpful, but you must dedicate time and effort to learning and practicing.
