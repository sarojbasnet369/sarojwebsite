The use of AI for web designing

Published on October 16, 2024

I designed this website using ChatGPT and Notepad. It’s hosted for free on GitHub (which supports static websites), and the ".com.np" domain was also freely available.

In today's digital world, artificial intelligence (AI) is changing how we design websites. Tools like ChatGPT help designers and developers make attractive and user-friendly websites more easily.

One big advantage of using AI in web design is that it can quickly come up with design ideas. By looking at existing designs and what users like, AI can suggest options that fit current trends and best practices.

For my personal website, I used ChatGPT to help with the design. It helped me think of content ideas, improve the layout, and make the overall user experience better. The final result is a clean and organized website that effectively showcases my skills and projects.

As AI continues to develop, I believe it will become even more important in web design. It will allow creators to focus more on their creativity and strategies while letting technology handle the more routine tasks.
