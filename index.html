About Me

I am Saroj Basnet, a 4th-year Mechanical Engineering student and an independent candidate running for Treasurer in the FSU Elections at IOE Pulchowk Campus.

Since my first year, I have been creating videos to highlight student issues, raising awareness about the problems we face on campus. But now, I don’t just want to talk about the issues—I want to solve them.

Why Independent?

✅ Financial Transparency – Every rupee spent will be accounted for.

✅ Student Welfare First – Better food, water, and campus facilities.

✅ Opportunities for All – More scholarships, research funding, and startup support.

✅ No Party Influence – I answer only to the students, not to party leaders.

Agendas

    • Financial Transparency & Accountability
      • For any program organized by FSU, the expense details and bills will be made public within one week to ensure transparency.
      • आर्थिक कार्यविधि (Financial Procedures) will be clearly outlined for students.
    • Fostering Entrepreneurship & Innovation
      • Startup culture introduction to encourage student-led ventures.
      • Promotion of research and innovation by supporting projects, funding opportunities, and collaboration.
      • Master’s and Bachelor’s collaboration for thesis work to enhance knowledge sharing and mutual benefit.
    • Student Welfare & Campus Facilities
      • Quality food in the canteen—students shouldn’t have to go outside, wasting time and extra money.
      • Clean drinking water and tap water in hostels, Robotics Club, Incubation Center, and other key areas.
      • Toilet management across campus, including proper facilities in the playground area.
      • Mental health sessions to support student well-being.
    • Sports & Extracurricular Activities
      • Ground management and sports events to be organized for all students, encouraging participation.
    • Financial Aid & Scholarships
      • Scholarships, study materials, and incentives will be provided to deserving students, considering financial need.
      • Searching for external opportunities and scholarships and ensuring they reach all students.
    • Career Development & Industry Connection
      • The FSU will act as a bridge between students and companies to facilitate internships and guest lectures.
    • Digital Transformation & Internet Accessibility
      • Focus on internet accessibility and campus digitalization to improve learning and communication.
    • Accountability & Good Governance
      • Ensuring teachers and campus administration are more responsible and accountable to students.
      • Continuing the best initiatives from past FSU administrations while improving on areas of weakness.
    • Building a Campus Worth Studying In
      • Our ultimate goal: Making IOE Pulchowk a better place to learn and grow.

    I will take responsibility for tasks within my position, work together on tasks that require collaboration, and advocate to the campus and government for matters beyond the FSU's capacity.

    The Difference

    In political parties, candidates must follow orders from upper leaders to secure a ticket. This means they are liable to those above them, not just the students. Their decisions are often influenced by party agendas rather than student needs. No matter how capable a person is, if they are in a party, they must follow orders from above.

    However, an independent representative is free from such pressure. They are not controlled or answerable to any higher political figures—only to the students who elect them. This allows them to make independent decisions that truly benefit the student body.

    We are striving to make the system work correctly—fairly and impartially—without bias towards political allies, relatives, or favoritism. Decisions should be based on merit and need, as they rightfully should be.

    Students should have the right to choose their own representative—one who prioritizes them over party politics.

    Be the Change. Vote for a Transparent and Student-First FSU!
