Here is a showcase of my key projects and work in mechanical engineering design and analysis:

  • Design of a Modular, Versatile, Frugal, Small-Scale Agricultural Machine for Cash Crop Cultivation in Nepal.

    In 24-hour 3D Design Hakathon, together with my peers I designed cash-crops planting, seeding machine. Features:

    • Multi-functionality: Ability to perform multiple farming tasks with interchangeable modules.
    • Adaptability: Operates smoothly in both dry and wet soil conditions.
    • Affordability: Low production and maintenance costs using local resources.
    • Ease of Use: Simple controls suitable for farmers with minimal technical expertise.
    • Scalability: Base design can be enhanced with automation features in the future.
