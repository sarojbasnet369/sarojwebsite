About Me

Hi, I’m Saroj Basnet, a Mechanical Engineering student in my 4th year at Pulchowk Campus. My academic journey has shaped my passion for mechanical design, especially in areas like 3D design, fluid simulation, and machine design. Alongside my studies, I have gained valuable practical experience through internships, most notably at Gorkha Brewery, where I worked on optimizing production processes.

I am deeply fascinated by traditional engineering challenges, such as the modernization of local Nepali alcohol (Raksi) production, and I’ve directed this interest into my final year project titled 'Fabrication of Modified Distillation Apparatus by CFD Analysis of Distillation Process of Traditional Nepali Alcohol, Raksi.

In addition to my engineering pursuits, I run a YouTube channel dedicated to helping students prepare for entrance exams for engineering programs in Nepal. I also work on various side projects, including the development of a Plastic Injection Molding Machine and creating a platform to share my 3D design and machine modeling work.

For Your Information, My blood group is A+ve.

Projects

Fabrication of Plastic Injection Molding Machine

Collaborated with peers to design and fabricate a plastic injection molding machine intended for the production of keyrings.

Fabrication of Modified Distillation Apparatus

Developing a modified distillation apparatus by conducting CFD analysis on the distillation process of traditional Nepali alcohol, Raksi.

  • Utilized computational fluid dynamics (CFD) for process optimization.
  • Designed an apparatus that enhances yield and quality of distillate.
  • Integrated traditional techniques with modern engineering principles.
